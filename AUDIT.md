### UX Audit

#### Issues in Live demo:

1. First issue:
	- Issue: The guiding dot leads the user to the crosshair in the middle, but it is not optimized. The user must move awkwardly in opposite (x-axis) directions.
	- Solution: Reverse the x axis movement. Keep the dot fixed at the center of the nose so it aligns with the center of the face on both axes.

2. Second issue:
	- Issue: The mouth guide is confusing and too small.
	- Solution: Use a horizontal oval. It can expand into a circle when the mouth is fully open and better matches natural movement.

3. Third issue:
	- Issue: The face guide is a full circle, which is unrealistic.
	- Solution: Use an oval since most face shapes are not perfectly circular.

4. Fourth issue (minor):
	- Issue: Results are lost if the tab is closed.
	- Solution: Add export to PDF so users can save results and retain company branding.

### Extras

- Background color (#4ebff7) with white text (#ffffff) is harsh on the eyes and may reduce readability for some users
- How it works link in the footer does not point to the correct section but works in the nav bar
- Email label and value in the footer do not match and may confuse users
